Welcome!

This upcoming MLB season is the most anticipated and exciting for the San Diego Padres and their fanbase. The “small market” franchise gained national attention and popularity within the past three years as they break bank accounts and the unwritten rules of baseball.

Many baseball experts predict the San Diego Padres to win the 2023 World Series based on their sheer starpower in the offense and late relief pitching. Any team would be lucky to have Machado, Soto, or Bogaerts, and the Padres have them all on one roster. Tatis Jr. had a successful spring training this year and is already making some noise in the minor leagues for his wrist and shoulder rehab assignment. The league is not ready for his comeback.
